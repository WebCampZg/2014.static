About NSoft

NSoft is a dynamic software development company specialized in building advanced solutions for gaming and betting companies. Our team is made up of young and talented professionals from different areas of work including project management, programming, design, 3D modeling, etc.

The company was founded in 2008. by two passionate individuals whose innovative ideas managed to transform it into a market leader in only six years. Today, NSoft has more than 100 employees whose average age is 27, but don't let that number fool you. Despite our young age, we approach each and every project in a professional manner, ensuring that our clients' needs are always met.

Why work at NSoft?
Flexible working hours;
Distinctive corporate culture which is a perfect mix of serious work and serious fun;
Employee perks (free fruits, baby bonus, all of the latest gadgets at employee's disposal, our own cafe, game room, IT library, free movie tickets, monthly team building events and parties, employee recognition program and a lot more);
Chance to be a part of a dynamic growing business in a company that aims to go global.
