About Dobar kod

We’re a small, experienced and dedicated team of web developers, providing our clients with web app consulting and development. We pour our love into the things we build, take pride in the code quality, and measure success with client satisfaction and happiness.

We’ve worked with clients all over the world – from stealth-mode Silicon Valley startups to top Croatian news portals or Australian business agencies.

We build apps on top of Python/Linux web stack. Aiming to use the right tool for the job, we’re experienced in various Python web frameworks, JavaScript frameworks, SQL and NoSQL databases, and the cloud.
