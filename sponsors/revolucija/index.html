About Studio Revolucija

Studio Revolucija is a creative studio that specializes in design and production of visually impressive and technologically advanced digital solutions. Over the 11 years since our foundation, Revolucija has worked for various international clients ranging from automotive, banking, retail, telecom and entertainment industries. They are a group of geeks who have logged over 200.000 hours creating digital experiences for our clients and their customers. In all this time they have learned that they have NOT seen it all, and they do NOT know it all. This is why Revolucija is still very passionate about every new project, and is enthusiastic to deliver results they are proud of. Revolucija is currently looking to expand their team of developers.

Studio Revolucija is hiring

Studio Revolucija and its IoT spinoff Amodo

We are looking for 3 software engineers (senior and junior) to join our team and work on innovative and international digital projects. Revolucija offers a wide range of digital interaction projects to work on (web and mobile apps, beacons and wearables), and Amodo is focused on its Connected Car solution that is about to hit the European market.
