Supporter packs

Continuing with tradition to provide free tickets and enable everybody to join our ever growing community, this year we are introducing Supporter packs - the awesome in small bags!

Beyond a cool t-shirt and "larger than life" stickers, other goodies will also be included in this small bag. Although we all love t-shirts and stickers, you'll really be helping us move this conference in a new direction and make it better for everybody.

Also, cool swag is cool ;-)

Of course, conference ticket is included with other goodies in Supporter pack.

If you want to help us make this conference even better leave your email and we'll inform you what's included and how you can get it.
